Meeting Scheduler is Mixmax integration that handles scheduling meetings in different timezone efficiently. Just select your and attendee's location, and integration will do the rest.

Features

Mixmax Integration

Works seamlessly with Mixmax slash commands.

No more back and forth

With one command scheduling, book meetings in one email instead of ten.

GCal support

Integration will suggest only the available slots from your calender.
